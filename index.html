ZERO-G
  • Zero-G is an annual 2-day cultural festival held at a college to celebrate art, culture, and diversity. It started in 2014 and was previously called Concordia. The festival features a variety of events such as music concerts, dance performances, theater plays, art exhibitions, fashion shows, and food fairs. Notable celebrities have attended the festival in the past. The aim is to provide a holistic experience for students, faculty, and staff.
